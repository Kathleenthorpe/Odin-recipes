Noodles

Varieties of instant noodles

Ingredients

  • Pack of instant noodles, pick your favourite
  • Boiling Water
  • Brocolli (optional)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sriracha

Steps

  1. Boil Water in a pan big enough to hold the noodles
  2. Add the noodles to the water
  3. Add brocolli to the mix
  4. Wait for everything to start boiling and doing stuff
  5. Add the flavour packets from the noodles*
  6. If you like it like me, empty everything into a bowl and add the sauces
  7. *If you like to drain your nooldes do that before adding saachets. Add them in the bowl.
  8. Enjoy
