French Toast

Fluffy French Toast

Ingredients

  • Thick white bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter (for the pan)

Toppings

  • Golden or Maple Syrup
  • Jams
  • Butter
  • Add any toppings that take your fancy

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl
  2. Add milk depending on consistency
  3. Season with salt if preferred
  4. Saturate the bread in the egg mix
  5. Add butter to a hot pan
  6. When the butter has melted and is hot add the bread to the pan
  7. Flip the toast when the underside starts to turn brown
  8. Serve with the topping of your choice
